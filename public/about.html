About the AI Text Summarizer App

           

                This application is designed to help users quickly generate summaries from long pieces of text. The summarizer uses advanced AI techniques to condense large bodies of text into their most essential points.                 It is a powerful tool for students, researchers, and professionals looking to save time while extracting key information.
